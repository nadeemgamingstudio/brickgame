Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Brick Game ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Important: Brick Game is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from our users.

Our primary goal is to provide an entertaining gaming experience without compromising your privacy.

Information We Collect

Brick Game collects minimal information necessary for the app to function properly:

  1. Non-Personal Information: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app performance.
  2. Game Progress: Your game scores and settings are stored locally on your device.
  3. Advertising Data: Google Admob may collect anonymous data for ad personalization (see Third-Party Services).

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Contact lists or messages
  • Location data
  • Payment information

Third-Party Services

Brick Game uses Google Admob to display advertisements. Admob may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Crash reports
  • Performance data

Google Admob Privacy Policy: You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party services.

Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your minimal usage data, and we are committed to protecting it:

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • We implement reasonable security measures
  • No personal data transmission occurs
  • No login or account creation required

However, remember that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Brick Game is suitable for all ages:

  • No age restriction for gameplay
  • No collection of personal information from children
  • Parental guidance recommended for in-app advertisements
  • Simple, non-violent gameplay suitable for all ages

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.
