Privacy Policy Overview

Welcome to Brick Game! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe gaming experience.

Important Notice: Brick Game is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from our users. Our goal is to provide pure entertainment without compromising your privacy.

By using Brick Game, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy from time to time, and we will notify you of any significant changes.

Information Collection

Brick Game collects minimal information necessary for optimal gameplay:

  • Game Progress Data: Scores, levels, and settings are stored locally on your device
  • Anonymous Usage Statistics: Basic analytics to improve app performance (non-identifiable)
  • Device Information: Basic device specs for compatibility optimization
We DO NOT Collect: Personal identification information, email addresses, contact lists, location data, payment information, or any sensitive personal data.

All game data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers unless specifically required for app functionality.

Third-Party Services

Brick Game uses Google Admob for displaying advertisements. Google Admob may collect:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Advertising ID (resettable by user)
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports for debugging
Google Admob Privacy Policy: You can review Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy

We have implemented appropriate measures to ensure that third-party services comply with privacy standards. However, we are not responsible for their independent privacy practices.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented appropriate measures:

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • No personal data transmission occurs during gameplay
  • No login or account creation required
  • Advertising data is handled by Google's secure infrastructure
  • Regular security assessments and updates
Security Note: While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We continuously work to protect your information to the best of our ability.

If you have concerns about data security, please contact us immediately.

Children's Privacy

Brick Game is suitable for players of all ages:

  • No age restriction for gameplay
  • No collection of personal information from children
  • Parental guidance recommended for in-app advertisements
  • Simple, non-violent gameplay suitable for all age groups
  • Educational value in problem-solving and reflexes

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

For Parents: We encourage parents to monitor their children's app usage and enable appropriate device-level restrictions for advertising and in-app purchases.

Your Rights & Controls

As a Brick Game user, you have the following rights:

  • Access and view your locally stored game data
  • Reset game progress at any time
  • Control advertising preferences through device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Opt-out of personalized ads via device settings
Data Deletion: Since all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app will remove all game data. Cloud backup is not available as we don't collect personal information.

For any privacy-related requests or questions, please contact us using the information provided below.
